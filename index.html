Welcome to my LPs Hall of Fame...

Join me on a journey through musical history as we celebrate the enduring brilliance of these iconic albums and artists. Each of these records holds a very special place in my heart, bringing back memories, moments, thoughts and feelings. I reunited the LP records that bring back some of the best moments in my life! Now step into my personal Top 10, with a handpicked collection of my favorite albums, artists and classical tracks!


Well, let's get started then...
